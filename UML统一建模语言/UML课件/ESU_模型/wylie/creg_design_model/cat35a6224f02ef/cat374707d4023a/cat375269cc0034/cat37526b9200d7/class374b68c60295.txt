Contains information about a Primary Course Offering that is schedule-specific (i.e., information about a particular primary Course Offering as it appears on a particular Schedule).  For example, the grade the student received in the Course Offering, as well as the status of the Course Offering on the Schedule (e.g., has the Course Offering been enrolled in yet, or has it just been selected on the Schedule).  Remember, Students can only be enrolled in and receive a grade in a Primary Course Offering, as opposed to an alternate Course Offering.
